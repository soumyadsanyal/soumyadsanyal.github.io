Soumya D. Sanyal

I’m currently a Data Scientist in New York City at Applecart, where I architect and build end-to-end machine learning pipelines, and develop new techniques and internal products to support client use cases.

I was previously a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Computer Science department at the University of Missouri, where I worked on applied machine learning problems in social media data, and on developing mathematical techniques to reason about information security.

I received the Ph.D. in mathematics from the University of Missouri. My thesis advisor was Prof. S. Dale Cutkosky. I received the B.A. in economics from the University of Cambridge.

People often have trouble figuring out how to pronounce my name. A useful device I’ve come up with is the following: if you can say sew me a t-shirt, you’re pretty much there! If you’re still uncomfortable with it, you’ll be pleased to know that I’m not fussy about how it’s pronounced.
