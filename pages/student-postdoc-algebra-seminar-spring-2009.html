A Pelican Blog

The Student/Postdoc Algebra Seminar in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Missouri meets weekly in 312 Mathematical Sciences Building at 12pm on Thursdays.

Current projects include talks by seminar participants and problem sessions on exercises from Robin Hartshorne’s Algebraic Geometry.

Questions, comments, suggestions, titles, abstracts and requests to be added to the mailing list for this seminar are welcome at (email): soumya [dot] d [dot] sanyal [at] mizzou [dot] edu .
