Soumya D. Sanyal

The Reading Group in Commutative Algebra and Algebraic Geometry in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Missouri meets weekly in 326 Mathematical Sciences Building at 5pm on Tuesdays.

Current projects include talks by seminar participants and problem sessions on exercises from Robin Hartshorne's Algebraic Geometry. Questions, comments, suggestions, titles, abstracts and requests to be added to the mailing list for this seminar are welcome at (email): soumya [dot] d [dot] sanyal [at] mizzou [dot] edu .
