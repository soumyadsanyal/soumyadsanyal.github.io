A Pelican Blog

The Seminar in Representation Theory in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Missouri met twice weekly in 320A Mathematical Sciences Building at 11am on Mondays and Fridays, with some exceptional Wednesdays.

Projects included talks by seminar participants and problem sessions on exercises from J. P. Serre’s Linear Representations of Finite Groups.

Questions, comments, suggestions, titles, abstracts and requests to be added to the mailing list for this seminar are welcome at (email): soumya [dot] d [dot] sanyal [at] mail [dot] mizzou [dot] edu .
