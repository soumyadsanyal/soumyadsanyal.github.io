CHACO Seminar

Spring 2015

The CHACO Seminar in the Center for High Assurance Computing in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Missouri meets weekly in 319 Engineering Building North at 3.00pm on Thursdays.

The seminar consists of talks given by students, postdocs, and faculty on papers relevant to their current research interests, or on classic papers in security, verification, languages, and logic. Questions, comments, suggestions, titles, abstracts and requests to be added to the mailing list for this seminar are welcome at (email): sanyals [at] missouri [dot] edu [to Soumya D. Sanyal] or procteram [at] missouri [dot] edu [to Adam Procter].
