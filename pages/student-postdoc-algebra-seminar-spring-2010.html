Student Postdoc Algebra Seminar Spring 2010

The Student/Postdoc Algebra Seminar in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Missouri meets weekly in 320A Mathematical Sciences Building at 3pm on Thursdays.

Current projects include talks by seminar participants and problem sessions on exercises from Claudio Procesi's Lie Groups.

Questions, comments, suggestions, titles, abstracts and requests to be added to the mailing list for this seminar are welcome at (email): soumya [dot] d [dot] sanyal [at] mail [dot] mizzou [dot] edu .
