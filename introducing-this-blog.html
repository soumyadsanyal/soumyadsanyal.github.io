Introducing this blog

I've set up this blog in order to encourage me to write on research topics that I'm interested in, and on projects that I'm currently doing. I've found that teaching has been a great way of learning things well; this is a way to force me to explain what I'm working on in ways that are clear, detailed and relate to concrete goals.
